drop

This is the capstone project I made for my Masters in Product Design at The New School's Parsons School of Design. I had the honor of being selected to present it at AIGA's Fresh Grad event in June of 2024. You can watch a recording of that presentation below for a 5min rundown of Drop. Drop is a drawing app designed to help artists warm-up, get inspired, and loosen up a little–encouraging them to benefit from the joy and therapy of drawing regularly, even if quickly or “badly”. People can respond to a daily curated prompt by making a Drop (drawing alone) or by diving into a Pool (drawing with everyone).

youtube feature

This is a proposal for a new feature on Youtube that would allow you to download or create PDF versions of any video on the platform. This feature can be a means to create more accessible content on Youtube and could also manifest as a creative community-building exercise. You can browse user-made PDF versions of any video on Youtube, or create a PDF version of any video on Youtube.

tutor orial

The task was to re-imagine and improve the existing booking flow for Tutor Orial, a tutor booking service. I wanted the product itself to be an example of engaging teaching, lead by a maskot called Orial who embodies the idea of learning is fun with various interesting interactions and an interface that challenges the visual and tonal conventions of academic platforms.
