drop

This is the capstone project I made for my Masters in Product Design at The New School's Parsons School of Design. I had the honor of being selected to present it at AIGA's Fresh Grad event in June of 2024. You can watch a recording of that presentation below for a 5min rundown of Drop. Drop is a drawing app designed to help artists warm-up, get inspired, and loosen up a little–encouraging them to benefit from the joy and therapy of drawing regularly, even if quickly or “badly”. People can respond to a daily curated prompt by making a Drop (drawing alone) or by diving into a Pool (drawing with everyone).

tutor orial

The task was to redesign the booking flow for Tutor Orial, a tutor booking service. I wanted the product itself to be to become an example of engaging teaching, lead by a maskot called Orial who embodies the idea of “learning is fun” with various interesting interactions and an interface that challenges the visual and tonal conventions of academic platforms.
