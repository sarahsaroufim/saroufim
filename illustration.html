artworks

Introduction

I’ve developed and continue to refine a unique style of my own. I enjoy working within restrictions: in black and white, with the same line width, and with flat abstract contours. I like using minimalist parts to create a maximalist whole. My favorite things to draw are surreal self-portraits and patterns. I want a piece to be at once meaningful and honest in its contentual expression but also compositionally sound and impactful in its form. I believe every dot matters and should be placed with intention.

absolut

Introduction

My winning submission to the global Absolut Creative Competition 2019 by Absolut Vodka. I was selected among 7,500 entries by an esteemed panel of judges composed of Mickalene Thomas, Bose Krishnamachari and Aaron Cezar for my piece Don't Keep Things Bottled Up, a reminder to seek levity, stay sociable and talk about how you're feeling. I had the pleasure of being hosted in Stockholm for a celebration party where we of course drank vodka in my honor. My poster was also displayed for a few months in Warschauer Brücke, Berlin.
