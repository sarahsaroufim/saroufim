my artworks

I’ve developed and continue to refine a unique style of my own. I like to work within restrictions–in black & white, with the same line width, with flat abstract contours. I view these self-imposed guidelines as an opportunity to explore composition, balance and impact. My favorite things to draw are surreal self-portraits and patterns.

absolut

My winning submission to the global Absolut Creative Competition 2019 by Absolut Vodka. I was selected among 7,500 entries by an esteemed panel of judges composed of Mickalene Thomas, Aaron Cezar & Bose Krishnamachari for my piece Don't Keep Things Bottled Up. The poster was displayed in Warschauer Brücke, Berlin during the month of March 2020 (a great month in history).
