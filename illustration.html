my artworks

I’ve developed and continue to refine a unique style of my own. I like to work within retrictions–in black & white, with the same line width, with flat abstract contours. I view these self-imposed guidelines as an opportunity to explore composition, balance and impact. My favorite things to draw are surreal self-portraits and patterns.

absolut

My winning submission to the global Absolut Creative Competition 2019 by Absolut Vodka. I was selected by an esteemed panel of judges (Mickalene Thomas, Aaron Cezar and Bose Krishnamachari) among 7,500 entries for my piece about mental health awareness "Don't Keep Things Bottled Up". The poster was displayed in Warschauer Brücke, Berlin for the month of March 2020 (a great month in history).
