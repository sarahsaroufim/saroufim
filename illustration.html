my artworks

I’ve developed and continue to refine a unique style of my own. I like to work within restrictions–in black & white, with the same line width, with flat abstract contours. I view these self-imposed guidelines as an opportunity to explore composition, balance and impact. My favorite things to draw are surreal self-portraits and patterns.

absolut

My winning submission to the global Absolut Creative Competition 2019 by Absolut Vodka. I was selected among 7,500 entries by an esteemed panel of judges composed of Mickalene Thomas, Bose Krishnamachari & Aaron Cezar for my piece Don't Keep Things Bottled Up. The poster was displayed in Warschauer Brücke, Berlin during the month of March 2020 (a great month in history).

instagram stickers

Traditional animation for modern times. These are hand-drawn frame-by-frame animated stickers I made for use on Instagram stories. You can search for “saroufim” and use them through the GIF section of your Instagram story or find them on Giphy where they’ve amassed over 1.4 billion views.

smash gym

Smash is a gym in Lebanon that was looking for a facelift. I established the brand identity, illustrative & typographic styles to be applied across Smash’s social media posts, posters and interior vinyl wall decals.

dammeh

Publication design and illustrations for Dammeh (meaning Hug or Embrace in Arabic). Dammeh is a collection of 11 profoundly moving short stories written by renowned Lebanese author Zeinab Charafeddine and published by Snoubar Beirut. Big hug to Snoubar and Zeinab for trusting me with interpreting these beautiful personal writings.
